Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I have a case?

The best way is to book a consultation. We will review the facts, any documents you have and explain whether you have a viable claim.

What must I bring to my consultation?

Bring your identity document, any correspondence, contracts, medical reports, police case numbers and any other documents related to your matter.

How do legal fees work?

Fee structures depend on the nature and complexity of the case. We will explain the expected fees and possible cost arrangements upfront.

Do you handle urgent or after-hours matters?

Yes, we understand that legal problems do not always occur during office hours. Contact us telephonically for urgent assistance.
